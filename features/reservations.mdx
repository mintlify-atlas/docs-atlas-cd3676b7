## Overview

The reservation system allows users to book specific workstations for morning, afternoon, or full-day sessions. The interface features an interactive map showing real-time availability and a booking card for confirming your selection.

## Making a Reservation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a date">
    Use the date navigator at the top to choose from the next 2 business days (weekdays only). The system skips weekends automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View the interactive map">
    The workstation map shows 6 desks around a central table. Each desk displays:
    - **Green chair**: Fully available
    - **Yellow chair**: Partially available (one time slot taken)
    - **Red chair**: Fully occupied (all time slots taken)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check who's reserved">
    The right panel (desktop) or drawer (mobile) shows current reservations for each workstation, including user names and time slots.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your workstation">
    Click on an available desk. The chair will highlight with a glow effect. Desks 1, 3, and 6 include support monitors.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your time slot">
    In the booking card that appears:
    - **Mañana (AM)**: 8:00 am – 12:00 pm
    - **Tarde (PM)**: 1:00 pm – 5:00 pm
    - **Día completo (Full day)**: 8:00 am – 5:00 pm

    Unavailable time slots are greyed out.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm your reservation">
    Review your selection and click the "Reservar" button to complete your booking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reservation Rules

### Rotation Policy

<Warning>
You must rotate workstations between reservations. If your last active reservation was at Desk 3, you cannot book Desk 3 for your next reservation.
</Warning>

The system enforces rotation by:
- Checking your reservation history automatically
- Blocking your previous desk from selection
- Showing a rotation message with your last desk and date

**Example rotation message**:  
*"Tu última reserva fue el 12/03/2026 en el escritorio 3. Debes hacer rotación y elegir otro escritorio."*

### One Reservation Per Day

<Note>
You can only have one active reservation per day. If you already have a reservation, the system will prevent duplicate bookings.
</Note>

### Time Restrictions

**Early morning limitation**:  
Between 12:00 AM and 5:00 AM, you cannot reserve for dates beyond tomorrow. This prevents late-night bookings too far in advance.

**Conflicting time slots**:  
- If someone books the AM slot, the "Full Day" option becomes unavailable
- If someone books the PM slot, the "Full Day" option becomes unavailable
- If someone books "Full Day," both AM and PM become unavailable

## Booking Card Details

### User Information

Your booking card displays:
- Your profile photo (or placeholder)
- Full name
- Job title (cargo)
- Department area

### Location Details

- **Workstation number**: "Escritorio 1" through "Escritorio 6"
- **Monitor badge**: Appears for desks 1, 3, and 6

### Time Slot Selection

Each time slot button shows:
- **Label**: Mañana, Tarde, or Día completo
- **Time range**: Exact hours for the shift
- **Availability**: Active/disabled state

## Real-Time Occupancy Panel

### Desktop View

The right panel shows all 6 workstations with:
- Current occupants (name + photo)
- Time slot badges (AM, PM, or "Todo el día")
- "Disponible" label for empty desks

### Mobile View

Tap the floating "Escritorios" button to open a drawer showing the same occupancy information.

<Info>
The occupancy panel updates automatically when reservations are made, ensuring you always see current availability.
</Info>

## Error Messages

### Common Validation Messages

| Message | Meaning |
|---------|----------|
| **"Ya tienes una reserva para este día"** | You already have an active reservation for the selected date |
| **"Este turno ya no está disponible"** | Another user just booked this time slot |
| **"Debes hacer rotación"** | You must choose a different desk from your last reservation |
| **"Este escritorio no tiene turnos disponibles"** | All time slots for this desk are taken |
| **"La ventana de confirmación no está activa"** | Reservation window is outside allowed hours |

### Time Restriction Message

<Warning>
"No puede reservar para pasado mañana (o fechas posteriores) entre 12:00 am y 5:00 am."
</Warning>

This appears when attempting to book beyond tomorrow during early morning hours (12:00 AM - 5:00 AM).

## Success Confirmation

After a successful reservation:
1. A "Reservado!" message appears in the booking card
2. The workstation map refreshes automatically
3. The booking card closes after 2.5 seconds
4. A custom event is dispatched to update other views

## Navigation Options

- **Date navigator**: Switch between available business days
- **Panel button**: View your reservations (or admin panel)
- **Logout button**: Sign out of the system
- **Back button**: Return to room selection

## Visual Legend

At the bottom of the reservation page:

- **Disponible (Green)**: Workstation has time slots available
- **Limitado (Yellow)**: Workstation is partially booked
- **Ocupado (Red)**: Workstation is fully booked for the day
- **Monitor icon**: Indicates which desks (1, 3, 6) have monitors

<Note>
The system automatically filters out weekends when generating available dates, showing only Monday through Friday.
</Note>
